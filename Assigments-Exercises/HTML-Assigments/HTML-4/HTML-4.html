Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby

  • Music

    • Steps to Get Started

    1. Choose an Instrument or Focus: Decide whether to sing, play an instrument, or produce music.
      Start with something accessible, like a keyboard or guitar.
    2. Learn Fundamentals: Use beginner courses or apps to understand scales, rhythm, and basic theory.
    3. Practice Daily: Dedicate a set time to practice and improve consistency.
  • Film

    • Steps to Get Started

    1. Watch Critically: Start with classic or popular movies and analyze their storytelling, cinematography, and editing.
    2. Learn the Craft: Explore free resources or courses on scriptwriting, directing, or editing.
    3. Experiment Creatively: Shoot short clips or scenes with your phone or a basic camera to practice.
  • Chess

    • Steps to Get Started

    1. Learn the Basics: Understand the rules, piece movements, and objective of the game. Use online tutorials or beginner guides.
    2. Practice Regularly: Play against AI or online opponents to build confidence and improve strategies.
    3. Study Games: Watch tutorials or analyze famous games to learn tactics and patterns.
